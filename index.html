CUTE ROADMAP


CUTE is a modular roadmap that may only consider one module initially and expand to meet the needs of the particular institution.
The basis of each module is the task / action (defined clearly and unambiguosly). The modules will be organized in a timeline and related to each other by the DIGICOMPEDU area(s).
  • All
  • Empowering Learners
  • Facilitating Learner
  • Assessment
  • Teaching and Learning
  • Digital Resources
  • Professional Engagement
